My History

Since I was a kid, I've always been passionate about games. My first console was an Atari, and with it came my love for the world of gaming. I've always found it fascinating and magical.

Later, while working at a local LAN house in my hometown, I had my first real contact with programming by setting up a private Ragnarok server. That experience opened my eyes to how games actually work behind the scenes—it was incredible.

When it was time to apply for college, there were no courses related to game development, so that dream had to be put on hold for a while. But one day, a new program opened in my city: a degree in Digital Game Technologies at Univali (Universidade do Vale do Itajaí). I didn't think twice and joined one of the first classes of the course.

Around the same time, I started working in the legal software department at a company called Fontec, where they were developing a gaming app called GameVelvet (also known as Megajogos in Brazil). My dream of working with games was getting closer, but still not quite there. Then, after graduating, I was invited to join the GameVelvet team, developing new games and assets for the app.

After years working with ActionScript 3 and Adobe Animate, I finally decided it was time to take the next big step in my career. I started diving deep into Unity, creating several personal projects as well as coursework projects.

This portfolio is a small showcase of these projects.

Now, I'm looking for new challenges and opportunities. I want to specialize even further in Unity and keep improving my skills with the goal of starting an international career in game development.

If you've made it this far—thank you for reading my story. I hope you enjoy exploring my projects!
